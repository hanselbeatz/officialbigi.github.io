ABOUT

The story behind the music

Official Bigi is a Kenyan artist blending faith, culture, and modern sound. Rooted in real life experiences, his music speaks to resilience, purpose, and identity.

From Nairobi to global platforms, Official Bigi continues to build a sound that is authentic, intentional, and deeply personal. Every release reflects growth, faith, and creative freedom.

“Faith over fame. Purpose over pressure.”
