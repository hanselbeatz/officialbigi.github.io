Cookies Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to improve your browsing experience. They help websites remember your preferences and analyze how visitors interact with the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We may use cookies to:

  • Improve website functionality
  • Analyze traffic and performance
  • Remember visitor preferences
  • Display embedded content (YouTube, Spotify, etc.)

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Needed for the website to operate.
Analytics Cookies: Measure how visitors use the website.
Third-Party Cookies: From platforms like YouTube, Spotify, TikTok, etc.

4. Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. However, some features may not work correctly if cookies are disabled.

5. Third-Party Services

External services integrated into this website (Spotify, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, etc.) may set their own cookies. We do not control how these platforms handle cookies.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Continued use of the website means you accept the changes.

7. Contact

If you have any questions, reach out via the Contact page.
